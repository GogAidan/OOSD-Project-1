Welcome to Revendrath


Revendrath may be difficult to get to but the traversal through the Shadowlands to this charming destination will be well worth it for any traveler; Adventurer or not. Our location boasts a plethora of attractions including the incridible feast fit for kings hosted by the dashing Price Renfell. Boasting amazing architecture and a traditional populace Revendrath will be certain to be your dream destination. Whether you choose to mingle with the residents or explore the wonderous outdoors beneath the full moon Revendrath will be sure to have a little something for everyone. Available October 1st until November 2nd!

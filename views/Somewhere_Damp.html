Welcome to the Swamp of Sorrows


The Swamp of Sorrows is located east of Deadwind Pass and rests near the Blasted Lands. In sharp contrast to its name-sake the Swamp of Sorrows will bring any adventurer worth their salt a worthy challenge and a deeply satisfying accomplishment. Our location boasts a plethora of attractions including the dreaded Temple of Atal'Hakkar for adventurers excited to test their mettle against the denizens that reside beneath. Whether you choose to dive below the surface and explore the majesty of the Sunken Temple or traverse through the mire the Swamp of Sorrows has a little something for everyone. Available March 4th until July 2nd!

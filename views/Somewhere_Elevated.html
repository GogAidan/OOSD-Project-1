Welcome to Irongforge


IronForge, the ancestral home of the Dwarves, is a true classic destination and a testament to majesty. way up in the mountains our location boasts a plethora of attractions including a guided tour of The Great Forge and a brisk mountain hike leadd by specialist agents. Whether you choose to delve deep into the depths of the mountain to witness the incredible engineering built beneath IronForge or traverse through the mountain trails beneath a gentle snowfall, IronForge has a little something for everyone. Available May 17th until September 4th!

Welcome to Elwynn Forest


Elewynn Forest is located upon the Eastern Kingdoms and boasts deep and vibrant woods. Our location lies deep within the Elderwoods and provides adventurers with easy access to the surrounding small hamlets allowing the hustle and bustle of the citylife to fade into the slower paced ease of small town life. Whether you choose to explore the woods communing with the wildlife or meet the easygoing and friendly residents of the surrounding hamlets Elwynn Forest has a little something for everyone.

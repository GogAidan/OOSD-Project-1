Welcome to the Stranglethorn Vale


The Stranglethorn Vale is located on the southern tip of the Eastern Kingdoms and boasts one of our warmest destinations. Perfect for anyone hoping to experience summer at any time of year, our location boasts a plethora of attractions and incredible sights to see. Whether you choose to explore the surrounding jungles to the west or swim in the crystal clear ocean to the south Stranglethorn Vale has no shortage of relaxing activities and a little something for everyone. Available June 4th until August 10th!

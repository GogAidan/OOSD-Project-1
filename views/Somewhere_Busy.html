Welcome to Dalaran


Dalaran is located on the west side of Northrend but it couldn't be any more unique from the surrounding area. Northrend may be a Northern Adventurers dream come true but Dalaran boasts the largest number of attractions for Adventurers more interested in the hustle and bustle of a major city. Our location boasts a plethora of attractions that put all other major cities to shame including the legendary Great Council of Mages. Whether you choose to explore the sights of the city during the day or experience the delights of a city alight with the setting sun Dalaran has a little something for everyone. Available all year!
